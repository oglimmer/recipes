# Chili con carne

### Fry in pan

* 500g of ground beef/pork mix

### Add to a large pot

* 5 table spoons of olive oil
* 3 cans of peeled tomatos
* one large onion, chopped
* one garlic, chopped
* 3 bell peppers, chopped

=> Cook for 45 minutes.

### Add and cook for 10 minutes

* can of corn
* can of kidney beans
* can of pinto beans
* can of black beans

### To taste

* salt/ regular black pepper
* tabasco
* Cayennepfeffer
