
# Pancakes

## Original recipe

* 2 cups all-purpose flour
* 2 teaspoons baking powder
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 3 tablespoons sugar
* 2 large eggs, lightly beaten
* 3 cups buttermilk
* 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted, plus 1/2 teaspoon for griddle

## German conversion

* 150 g flour
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 1 teaspoon baking soda
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 20g tablespoons sugar
* 1 egg
* 240 ml buttermilk
* 30g melted butter

(~1060 kcal)

## Instructions

* beat eggs
* add melted butter
* add buttermilk
* add flour + baking soda + baking powder + sugar + salt
* stir very carefully!  go lumpy!
* fry on low heat until bumbles are seen on the upper side
