# German meat balls (aka Buletten)

## Buletten

* 500g of ground beef/pork mix
* 2 eggs
* 2 german buns (kaiser-bröchten)
* Milk ~0.3l
* Salt, pepper and paprika to taste
* 1/2 bulb of garlic
* 3/4 large onion
* mustard

## Tomato sauce

* 3 cans of peeled tomatos
* 3/4 large onion
* 1/2 bulb of garlic
* salt/pepper/italian herb
* olive oil
* tomato paste (1/2 tube)
